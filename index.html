Who We Are

A Little Corner of Sweet Tradition

Born from a love of classic Filipino desserts and the warmth of a cozy café, El Rincón del Flan is your go-to spot for silky, golden leche flan and freshly brewed coffee.

Every flan is lovingly made from scratch — creamy, caramel-kissed, and worth every spoonful.
